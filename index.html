ABOUT THE GAME

A small game with Big dreams

Parallax Paradox is a unique 2D Plataformer game where the player can switch between different layers of the map. Currently only has two full levels but the team is working hard to reach the full potential of the game.This two levels are the top of the iceberg that the game can be .The game is made by Marco Polo Productions, an association of two members, anxious of work and full of ideas with a goal which is to make the game a reality.
